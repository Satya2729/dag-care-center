üêï Dog Diseases and Prevention

  • Rabies: A deadly viral disease. Prevention: Regular vaccination.
  • Parvovirus: Affects puppies and causes vomiting and diarrhea. Prevention: Early vaccination and hygiene.
  • Distemper: Affects respiratory and nervous systems. Prevention: Annual vaccination.
  • Lyme Disease: Caused by tick bites. Prevention: Tick control and regular vet checks.
  • Heartworm: Spread by mosquitoes. Prevention: Monthly preventive medication.
  • Leptospirosis: Bacterial infection from contaminated water. Prevention: Vaccination and avoiding stagnant water.
